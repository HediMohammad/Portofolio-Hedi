Praktik Observasi Lapangan

Project achievements and my role in this project

Project Achievements

This academic activity focused on observing and analyzing destination marketing strategies to enhance students' practical understanding of the field.

  • Marketing strategy analysis in Yogyakarta: Studied how tourism actors attract visitors through promotion and local branding.
  • Comparison with Bandung strategies: Identified differences in approaches between Yogyakarta and Bandung regarding media, community involvement, and government support.
  • Recommendations for Bandung destinations: Provided actionable insights to help boost Bandung's tourism marketing.

My Role

In the Field Observation Practice program, I served as the of the Public Relations Division, responsible for establishing and maintaining communication between the organizing team and external parties such as speakers, tourism destination managers, and partner institutions. I also managed the publicity strategy for the program before, during, and after the observation activities. This role required strong interpersonal communication skills, negotiation abilities, and sensitivity in building professional relationships. I ensured that all external communication needs were fulfilled, including scheduling meetings, requesting observation permissions, and preparing documentation for reporting and social media publication purposes.
