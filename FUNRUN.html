Communication Fun Run

Project achievements and my role in this project

Project Achievements

This sports-based community event aimed to promote healthy living while fostering togetherness among students and the public.

  • Successfully engaged dozens of participants: Attracted students, lecturers, alumni, and locals with strong enthusiasm.
  • Promoted a positive image of the Communication program: Helped introduce the program in a fun, engaging way.
  • Fostered solidarity and collaboration: Strengthened participant bonds and encouraged teamwork.
  • Professionally managed by students: Showcased strong planning and event management skills through full student-led organization.
  • Attracted local sponsors: Gained support from businesses in the form of prizes, food, and merchandise.

My Role

In the Communication Fun Run event, I served as the Chief Executive (Event Leader), overseeing the entire process from planning and execution to post-event evaluation. I coordinated all organizing divisions, including licensing, logistics, sponsorship, publicity, and technical operations on the event day. As the leader, I ensured that each team carried out its duties on time and managed any issues that arose with quick and practical solutions. I also maintained strong communication with faculty supervisors and sponsors to ensure smooth operations and full support for the event. This role required firm yet adaptable leadership, strong managerial abilities, and effective communication skills to ensure that all aspects of the event ran smoothly and delivered a meaningful experience for all participants.
