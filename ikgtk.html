Ilmu Komunikasi Goes To Kampoeng

Project achievements and my role in this project

Project Achievements

This community service program involved Communication Science students engaging directly with residents at the neighborhood (RW) level to raise awareness about healthy living and environmental care.

  • Environmental awareness and waste sorting education: Educated residents on keeping the environment clean and sorting organic and inorganic waste.
  • Child nutrition awareness session: Delivered information on balanced nutrition for children's development through engaging and simple materials.
  • Free medical check-up: Offered basic health checks like blood pressure and sugar level tests, promoting routine health monitoring.
  • Neighborhood Festival (Pesta Rakyat): Hosted a local celebration to wrap up the program and strengthen community spirit.

My Role

As the Chief Executive (Event Leader), I held full responsibility for planning, coordinating, and executing the entire series of activities. I led the organizing team, delegated tasks based on divisions, and ensured that every part of the program ran according to the timeline and objectives. I also served as the main liaison between the committee, academic advisors, sponsors, and participants. This role required strong leadership, effective communication skills, and the ability to make quick and accurate decisions. I was actively involved in designing the event concept, resolving on-site challenges, and ensuring that every activity ran smoothly and delivered meaningful impact to all stakeholders.
